## What is registered

This record contains SHA-256 hashes of the FTS26 pre-registered analysis plan (v1) and
survey instrument (v1). The documents themselves are held privately until fielding closes
because the analysis plan contains numeric anti-manipulation trigger values that would be
gameable if published in advance. The full plan — byte-identical to these hashes — will be
published in the survey report's methodology appendix within 30 days of fielding close.

Anyone can then verify: `sha256sum <published file>` must equal the hash below, proving the
plan was not modified after this registration date.

## Amendment policy

Before fielding begins, the plan may be superseded only by an explicit, dated v2 amendment
committed to this repository (new hashes, old record preserved). Once fielding begins, no
changes to thresholds or detection triggers are permitted; any post-fielding deviation is
disclosed in the published report.
